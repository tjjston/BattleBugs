ℹ️ How P4P is Calculated

Pound-for-pound ranks bugs by dominance within their tier — tiers are treated equally. A perfect ZU champion beats an average Uber champion.

  • Champions: 300 base + win rate (×100) + 25 per title defense.
  • Contenders: position score (rank 1 = 145 pts, sliding down) + win rate (×60).
  • No tier multiplier — a ZU #1 contender with a 100% win rate ranks above an Uber #1 with losses.
