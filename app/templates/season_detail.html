How Seasons Work
  1. Register your bug during the registration window.
  2. The regular season runs for 7 days — your bug plays one auto-match per day at noon UTC.
  3. After each match you earn boost points: +3 for a win, +1 for a loss.
  4. Assign boost points to raise a stat permanently before your next match, or set an auto-assign stat to handle it automatically. Unassigned points are randomly distributed right before the next match starts.
  5. Top performers by win record advance to the season-ending playoff tournament.
⚡ Boost Points — Full Tutorial

Boost points let you permanently improve one of your bug's six stats after each regular-season match. Here's how to use them:

1. Earning points
Every match awards boost points regardless of outcome:
  • Win → +3 boost points
  • Loss → +1 boost point
Points accumulate as pending — they don't do anything until assigned.
2. Assigning manually
Find your bug in the Standings table and click Boost. Select a stat (Attack, Defense, Speed, Lethality, Grip, or Cunning) and click Apply. All pending points go to that stat in one shot — there's no splitting.
3. Auto-assign
Set an Auto: [stat] preference and points are applied automatically right after each match ends. Change it anytime — even mid-season. Clear it (set to manual) to go back to deciding yourself.
4. The deadline
If you haven't assigned or set auto-assign before your next match begins, the system randomly distributes all pending points across your stats. You won't lose the points — they just go wherever the arena decides.
5. Strategy tips
  • Dumping points into your dominant stat (the one your type relies on) compounds type advantages.
  • Patching a weakness (e.g. raising Cunning if your matchups are bad) can flip results.
  • Lethality amplifies type-advantage damage — boosting it makes favorable matchups dominant.
  • Grip helps control evasive opponents; Cunning reduces damage when you're at a type disadvantage.
Stat changes from boost points are permanent — they carry forward to tournaments and all future battles.
